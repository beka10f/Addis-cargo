How It Works

  • Contact us to schedule a pickup for your shipment.
  • Our team will come to your desired location and collect the items.
  • We will transport your shipment to our drop-off locations in Addis Ababa.
  • Our dedicated colleagues will receive your items and securely store them until they are ready for transit.
  • Upon the arrival of your shipment, you have the option to personally pick it up or arrange for delivery to your address.

For more information or to schedule a shipment, contact us and we'll assist you every step of the way.

About Us

Addis-Cargo is a logistics startup dedicated to serving the Ethiopian diaspora. We understand the importance of affordable shipping, reliable delivery, and convenience for our customers. With our expertise in the logistics industry, we aim to provide exceptional service and bridge the gap for the Ethiopian community.

Our team is committed to ensuring your shipments are handled securely and delivered on time. We take pride in being a trusted partner for your shipping needs.
