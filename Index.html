How It Works

  • Contact us to schedule a pickup for your shipment.
  • Our professional team will collect your items from your desired location.
  • Your shipment will be transported to our drop-off locations in Addis Ababa.
  • Our colleagues will receive and securely store your items until they're ready for transit.
  • Once your shipment arrives, you can pick it up or arrange for delivery to your address.

For more information or to schedule a shipment, contact us and we'll assist you every step of the way.
