How It Works

Step 1: Contact Us

Reach out to us to discuss your dollar needs for importing goods.

Step 2: Product Selection

We arrange a meeting at your store, where you can carefully choose the items you want to order.

Step 3: Payment in Birr

Once you've made your selections, we will collect the payment in Birr for the chosen products.

Step 4: Purchase and Shipment

After receiving your payment, we will proceed to purchase the products on your behalf and arrange for their shipment to Ethiopia.

Step 5: Customs Clearance and Delivery

When the shipment arrives in Ethiopia, we take care of the customs clearance and deliver the products directly to your store.
